About Me - Sarah Barter

Sarah Barter

I'm originally from Ireland but have been living in different places, mostly around Europe, since 2013. I'm currently based in Vigo, in the north-west of Spain. I'm very new to the coding world since joining SheCodes in October, 2022 but I've loved every minute of it!

My studies

I graduated from Trinity College, Dublin in 2012 with a degree in History of Art and Architecture and Italian. I then completed a course to teach English as a foreign language and moved to Spain. I've lived and worked in various cities around Spain, Oviedo, Carmona, San Sebastian and Vigo. I also lived in Belgrade, Serbia for a year where I studied for a Master's in Cultural Policy and Management.
