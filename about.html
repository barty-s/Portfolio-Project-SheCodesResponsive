About Me

Irish front-end developer student with SheCodes, based in Vigo, Spain

Sarah Barter

I'm originally from Ireland but have been living in different places, mostly around Europe, since 2013. I'm currently based in Vigo, in the north-west of Spain. I'm very new to the coding world since joining SheCodes in October, 2022 but I've loved every minute of it!

My studies

I graduated from Trinity College, Dublin in 2012 with a degree in History of Art and Architecture and Italian. I then completed a course to teach English as a foreign language and moved to Spain. I've lived and worked in various cities around Spain. I also lived in Belgrade, Serbia for a year where I studied for a Master's in Cultural Policy and Management. My lastet educational challenge is with SheCodes, learning to be a Front-End Developer.

My Work Experience

I've been teaching English as a foreign language since 2013. I've mainly worked in Spain in cities such as Oviedo, Carmona, San Sebastian (pictured), and currently, Vigo. I've also worked in Ireland and the UK on summer courses. I'm now looking to move into the tech industry and, hopefully, make a career change...here's to new challenges in 2023!

My Hobbies

I've always loved water sports, from swimming to surfing to sailing. I took up windsurfing in 2019 and haven't looked back. Where I live has perfect conditions for windsurfing year-round and it's a great way to get out into nature, even in the depths of the stormy winter months, which is actually my favourite time of year to sail with stormy, windy conditions! I'm also into running, and have done three half-marathons so far.
